Our Story

Built by a champion,
for future champions.

Indian Amateur Roller Skating Academy was born from a simple belief — world-class training shouldn't be limited to a select few. Founded by S. Lakshman Rao, who captured the All India National Roller Skating Championship in 2005, IARSA has been an engine of athletic excellence across Andhra Pradesh for over two decades.

FAQ

Common questions,
clear answers.

Everything you need to know before your first session at IARSA.

Still have questions?

Call us or drop a message — we reply fast.

+91 99596 46165
We welcome students from age 5 and above. Our Beginners class is perfect for young children just discovering skating. There's no upper age limit — adults are welcome at all levels!
For your first session, we recommend coming without skates so coaches can assess the right type and size for you. We can guide you on the best skates to purchase for your level and discipline.
Our rink is at Shivaji Park, MVP Colony, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. It is easily accessible by public and private transport. Contact us for precise directions to the rink entrance.
Coaches monitor each student's progress continuously. When a student demonstrates stable balance, basic strides, and stopping control, our head coach will recommend progression. There's no fixed timeline — every student progresses at their own pace.
Absolutely. Our Senior Artistic program is specifically designed for competition-ready athletes. Our founder S. Lakshman Rao is himself a national champion and coaches students targeting district, state, and national competitions including those affiliated with the All India Roller Skating Federation.
Beginners train Tuesday–Sunday (Mon & Thu are holidays) at 5:00–6:30 AM and PM. Juniors Artistic train Monday–Friday 4:00–5:30 PM, with extended Sat/Sun morning sessions. Senior Artistic trains Monday–Friday 6:00–8:30 PM, with extended weekend sessions.
